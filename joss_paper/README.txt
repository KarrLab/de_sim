This directory contains a paper published by JOSS, Arthur P. Goldberg & Jonathan Karr. (2020). DE-Sim: an object-oriented, discrete-event simulation tool for data-intensive modeling of complex systems in Python. Journal of Open Source Software, 5(55), 2685, https://doi.org/10.21105/joss.02685.

At the time of publication, it was compiled into pdf by using the Whedon paper preview service at https://whedon.theoj.org/.

# Contributions

A.P.G. conceptualized, designed, and implemented DE-Sim.
J.R.K. provided motivation and input for DE-Sim and helped test, document, and distribute DE-Sim.
Both authors wrote and approved this paper.
